Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

At International Credit Union, we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of your personal information and maintaining your trust in our banking, financial, and digital services. We collect personal data including, but not limited to, your full name, date of birth, identification details, contact information, financial records, transaction history, credit information, employment details, device and technical data, usage information, communication records, and any other information necessary to provide our services. This information may be collected directly from you through account applications, transactions, communications, website and mobile app usage, or indirectly from credit bureaus, regulatory authorities, identity verification services, employers, or authorized third parties. We use your personal data to open and manage accounts, process transactions, provide loans and investment services, verify your identity, comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) and anti-money laundering regulations, assess creditworthiness, prevent fraud, manage risk, improve our services, respond to inquiries, send service-related communications, and, where permitted by law, provide marketing and promotional materials, which you may opt out of at any time. We process your information based on contractual necessity, legal obligations, legitimate business interests, and, where required, your consent. We do not sell your personal information; however, we may share it with trusted service providers, payment processors, IT vendors, auditors, credit reporting agencies, regulators, law enforcement authorities, courts, and other entities when required by law or in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or corporate restructuring, and all such parties are required to maintain appropriate confidentiality and security standards.

We implement comprehensive administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including encryption, secure servers, multi-factor authentication, access controls, monitoring systems, and regular security audits to protect your data, though no system can guarantee absolute security, and customers are encouraged to protect their credentials and notify us of suspicious activity. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill contractual and legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet regulatory retention requirements. Where data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are implemented in accordance with applicable data protection laws. Subject to local regulations, you may have the right to access, correct, update, restrict, object to, or request deletion of your personal information, withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, request data portability, or lodge a complaint with a relevant supervisory authority. Our website and mobile applications use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, improve performance, analyze usage patterns, strengthen security, and deliver relevant content, and you may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Our services are not intended for minors unless permitted by applicable law, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children without proper authorization. This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal, regulatory, or operational changes, and any updates will be published with a revised effective date.

If you have questions regarding this policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, you may contact our Data Protection Officer at International Credit Union , Stockton St San Francisco CA 94133-3816.
